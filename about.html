About Me

Hello, I'm Jonathan Lindstrom, a rising junior at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm pursuing a double major in Information Systems and Human Computer Interactions, with a minor in Intelligent Environments. I have a passion for designing front-end interfaces, like this website, and I'm fascinated by the way people interact with technology. My goal is to become a UX designer after completing my degree.

Beyond academics, I'm a member of Carnegie Mellon's varsity swim and dive team, specializing in springboard diving for the past seven years. The feeling of freedom and the adrenaline rush I experience while flipping and twisting through the air is truly exhilarating. Additionally, I'm actively involved in Phi Delta Theta, where I strive to build connections and meet new people, a goal I set for myself when I entered college.

At home, I have three brothers: Matthew (22), Tim (17), and Chris (15), and two dogs named Ollie and Perkins. Growing up in a large family was a wonderful experience for me, as there was always someone to talk to or spend time with. As a family, we share a love for travel, trying out exotic cuisines, and playing board games. Being one of four boys made it easy to form teams for games and competitions. Scuba diving is a passion we all share, and I hope we continue to embark on exciting adventures together as we grow
